Mission Statement

Nex Bangladesh’s core ideals lie in making a positive, transformative change in our society through our initiatives, campaigns, and events. Our aims include encouraging youth participation in global politics and spreading awareness about humanitarian issues, while also serving our community.

Vision Statement

We aim and strive for a new tomorrow, free from conflicts that divide us in today’s world. We believe that the young minds of today will be the leaders of the future. Hence, we understand that it is our responsibility to nurture these young minds in an environment fostering growth in a multicultural world.
